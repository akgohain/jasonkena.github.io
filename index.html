Jason Ken Adhinarta

I am a Gabelli Presidential Scholar at Boston College and a research assistant at Prof. Donglai Wei's Computer Vision lab, where I work on applying machine learning to computer vision problems, particularly in the field of connectomics.

Previously, I was a research intern at Emmerich Research Center in Indonesia where I was mentored by Dr. Eden Steven.
